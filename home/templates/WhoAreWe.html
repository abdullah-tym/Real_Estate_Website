A large Turkish company specializing in real estate consultancy and brokerage, located in Istanbul, Trabzon, has dealings in other Turkish cities, agents in several Arab cities, and numerous agreements with most of the major construction companies in Istanbul focusing on foreign real estate investors, who want to own in Turkey.
